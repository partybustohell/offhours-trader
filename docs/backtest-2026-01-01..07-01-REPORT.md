## 7. Root-cause findings — why zero trades

The null result is structural, not noise. Two independent mechanisms each suffice to prevent trading; both were isolated with the run's own artifacts.

### 7.1 The synthesis normalization ceiling (primary)

Weighted conviction divides the agreeing analysts' weight×conviction by the weight-sum of ALL responding analysts (`src/synthesis.ts`). All five analysts respond on every candidate (abstentions are `direction: none` verdicts, which count in the denominator, never the numerator). Consequence: two agreeing analysts at 0.65 conviction score ≈ 0.28; clearing the default 0.65 threshold requires 4–5 analysts in high-conviction directional agreement — which the deliberately abstention-friendly personas essentially never produce.

Measured over all ~1,050 verdicts in this run (real `computeThesisEntries`, threshold zeroed):

| threshold | candidates clearing (of 66 directional composites) |
|---|---|
| 0.45 | 1 (FDX long 0.467, 2026-03-19) |
| 0.50 | 0 |
| 0.65 (default) | 0 |

**The entire configurable threshold range (0.45–0.70) sits above the strategy's empirical conviction ceiling (0.467).** No weight tuning fixes this; it is the normalization semantics interacting with persona abstention discipline. Design options (a user decision, not a tweak): normalize over agreeing analysts with an agreement quorum; exclude `none` verdicts from the denominator; or recalibrate personas toward commitment.

### 7.2 Extended-hours quote blindness (independent secondary)

The executor's quote source is IEX (`getLatestQuotes`, production parity), but IEX operates 08:00–17:00 ET only. In the single episode that produced a thesis entry, all 37 extended-hours ticks skipped on `no quote`, and the two 08:00+ premarket ticks that found an IEX book skipped on a 1,032 bps spread (gate: 50 bps). **Production implication:** live, `getLatestQuotes` would return quotes that are stale by up to hours during 17:00–20:00 and 04:00–08:00 — the system would either not trade (with staleness checks) or trade on a book it cannot see (without them). Extended-hours execution needs a consolidated real-time quote feed (paid tier) or an explicit stale-quote policy.
